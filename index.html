Our Team

LPUFOODFINDS

With so many canteens, kiosks, and food options available on the LPU campus, the challenge is to decide what to eat while also saving the monthly pocket money as much as we can. Keeping this in mind, our team has come up with LPU FOOD FINDS- a food prices comparison portal where you can compare prices of a particular food item that is available at many spots inside the campus and find a place that is not harsh on your pockets!
